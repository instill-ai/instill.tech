**Versatile Data Pipeline (VDP)** is an open-source unstructured data Extract-Transform-Load (ETL) tool to streamline the end-to-end unstructured data processing pipeline with Artificial Intelligence (AI). Before we start, let's briefly introduce "Why we need VDP?"


## What is ETL

When people say they are data-driven, most of the time, it means structured data drive them. Although [80% of the world's data are unstructured](https://www.analyticsinsight.net/the-future-of-data-revolution-will-be-unstructured-data/), the reality is that unstructured data are more challenging to analyse. It requires Iterative ETL procedures (sometimes called data massage) to convert this unstructured data into its structured format ready for further analysis.

VDP streamlines the end-to-end unstructured Data processing pipeline: 

- **Extract** unstructured data (e.g., images) from pre-built data sources such as cloud/on-prem storage or IoT devices.
- **Transform** it into analysable data representations (e.g., labels) with AI models.
- **Load** the transformed data into warehouses, applications, or other destinations.

## Why VDP?

Due to the proliferation of AI, more and more developers and companies wish to include AI models in their ETL pipelines. Unfortunately, building reliable ETL pipelines can be challenging. Not many companies know or have the resources to deal with it. That's why we introduce [Versatile Data Pipeline (VDP)](http://go.instill.tech/4em2l4), **a general and modularised ETL infrastructure powered by AI for unstructured data**.

> We believe VDP is the future Artificial Intelligence ETL for unstructured data. 
>
> -- <cite> Instill AI Team</cite>

Developers won't need to build their data connectors, AI model serving platform (requiring much maintenance), and ELT-pipeline automation tools.*
