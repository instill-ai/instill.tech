## About

**☁️ [Instill Cloud](https://instill.tech)** is a fully managed platform hosted
in the public cloud, offering a variety of services for the **🔮 [Instill
Core](../core/introduction)** project. It encompasses a wide range of features
such as **💧 [Instill VDP](../vdp/introduction)**, **⚗️ [Instill
Model](../model/introduction)**, **💾 Instill Artifact (coming soon)**, and **🍎
Instill App (coming soon)**, collectively forming a complete AI solution stack.

By signing up for **☁️ Instill Cloud**, you gain one-click access to the
platform. We provide free **[Instill Credit](./credit)** to help you get started. You
can discover and interact with intriguing public pipelines and models on the
**[Instill Hub](./hub)**.
