**Instill Hub** in **☁️ Instill Cloud** provides a platform where you can
interact with and run pipelines and models developed by others, and also share
your own work. The **Hub** page acts as a unified repository for public
pipelines and models, facilitating their discovery and execution for users. All
public pipelines and models are available to everyone on the **Hub** page.

## Run Pipeline

To run another user's pipeline through the **Pipeline Preview** page on **Instill Hub**,
follow these steps:

1. Navigate to **Instill Hub** and locate a pipeline that interests you.
2. Click on the title of the pipeline to access the **Pipeline Preview** page.
3. Provide the necessary inputs or upload the required files to configure the
   pipeline.
4. Click the `Run` button to run the pipeline and receive the results.

## Clone Pipeline

If you find a pipeline that you would like to modify or use as a template for a
new pipeline, you can clone it. To clone a pipeline, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to **Instill Hub** and locate a pipeline that interests you.
2. Click on the title of the pipeline to access the **Pipeline Preview** page.
3. Click the `Clone` button.
4. In **Clone this pipeline** dialog, fill in the following details:

   - **Owner**: Determine the cloned pipeline to be under your personal account
     or the organization you belong to.
   - **Pipeline Name**: Assign a unique identifier to your new pipeline.
   - **Description**: Optionally, provide additional information about the
     pipeline.
   - **Visibility**: Determine whether your pipeline should be public or
     private.

5. Press `Clone` to complete the operation and begin utilizing or modifying the
   pipeline as necessary.
