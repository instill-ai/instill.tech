VDP is under rapid development. Our team is pushing new features every day to meet the needs of our users. Here is our public high-level roadmap til the end of 2023. If you are interested in what's being worked on right now and what will be explored in the future for the VDP project, please check our [VDP project roadmap](https://github.com/orgs/instill-ai/projects/5/views/4).

## 2022 Q4

- 🎯 [api-gateway] Add API Gateway / Reverse Proxy
- 🎯 [vdp] Support VDP Kubernetes deployment

## 2023 Q1

- 🎯 [vdp] Support multi-language SDKs
- 🎯 [vdp] Add dashboard for out-of-the-box monitoring
- 🔮 [model][console] Support model card
- ☁️ Instill Cloud (Alpha): our hosted service to provide you with all the power of VDP, without managing infrastructure. If you're interested, [join the waitlist now](https://www.instill.tech/get-access/?utm_source=documentation&utm_medium=link).

## 2023 Q2

- 🔌 [connector] Support `PULL` mode with new source connectors for scheduling pipeline
  - Local filesystem
  - Google Cloud Storage (GCS)
- 🎯 [vdp] Instill CLI
- 🎯 [vdp] Support logging
- 🎯 [logic] Support logic operator pipeline component
- 🔮 [model][automl] Support user dataset uploading
- 🔮 [model][automl] Support AI model iteration
- 🔮 [model][automl] One-click deploy fine-tuned AI models

## 2023 Q3

- 🔮 [model][automl] Support data labelling
- 🔮 [model] Model hub for discovery and sharing

## 2023 Q4

- 🎯 [pipeline] Support complex pipeline (DAG)
- 🎯 [vdp] SQL extension for dataset and model management
- 🔮 [model] Support model monitoring

## Contributing to the roadmap

Your feedback is important for us to understand how to build better and valuable tools for the community. If you'd like to **send us a feature request**, have a look at the [discussions](https://github.com/instill-ai/vdp/discussions) first. If your idea has already made to the planning, give it the thumbs up 👍 to vote for it. If not, you can create a [new discussion](https://github.com/instill-ai/vdp/discussions/new). Besides, you can also join our [community](https://discord.gg/sevxWsqpGh) and request it in the #vdp channel.
