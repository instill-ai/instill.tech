VDP is under rapid development. Our team is pushing new features every day to meet the needs of our users. Here is our public high-level roadmap for 2022. If you are interested in what's being worked on right now and what will be explored in the future for the VDP project, please check our [VDP project roadmap](https://github.com/orgs/instill-ai/projects/5/views/4).

### 2022 Q3

- 🎯 Add API gateway / Reverse Proxy
- 🎯 Add auth to endpoints
- 🎯 Support deploying VDP on Kubernetes
- 🔮 Improve the UX to import models
- ☁️ Instill Cloud (Alpha): our hosted service to provide you with all the power of VDP, without managing infrastructure. If you're interested, [join the waitlist](https://www.instill.tech/get-access/?utm_source=documentation&utm_medium=link).

### 2022 Q4

- 🔌 Support `FETCH` mode with new source connectors
  - Local filesystem
  - Google Cloud Storage (GCS)
- 🎯 Add dashboard for out-of-the-box monitoring and logging
- 🪢 Scheduling feature to control how often to trigger a pipeline
- ☁️ Instill Cloud (Alpha): our hosted service to provide you with all the power of VDP, without managing infrastructure. If you're interested, [join the waitlist now](https://www.instill.tech/get-access/?utm_source=documentation&utm_medium=link).

### Future

- 📺 Instill CLI
- 🎯 Support multi-language SDKs
- 🔮 AutoML service to automatically build, train and deploy ML models on VDP
- 🔮 Improve model deployment with the latest serverless technology
- 🔮 Support new embedding task: convert unstructured image data to low-dimensional vectors. The task is the foundation for real-world applications such as retrieval, semantic search and recommendation systems

## Contributing to the roadmap

Your feedback is important for us to understand how to build better and valuable tools for the community. If you'd like to **send us a feature request**, have a look at the [discussions](https://github.com/instill-ai/vdp/discussions) first. If your idea has already made to the planning, give it the thumbs up 👍 to vote for it. If not, you can create a [new discussion](https://github.com/instill-ai/vdp/discussions/new). Besides, you can also join our [community](https://discord.gg/sevxWsqpGh) and request it in the #vdp channel.
