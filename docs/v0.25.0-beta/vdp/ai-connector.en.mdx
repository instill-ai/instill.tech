An **AI Connector** serves as a critical pipeline component responsible for converting data into interpretable and analyzable formats,
simplifying the extraction of valuable insights.
These connectors establish links with AI models originating from diverse providers, be it the first-party ModelOps platform, **Instill Model**, third-party model vendors, or in-house model serving platforms.
