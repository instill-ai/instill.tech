🚀 We strongly believe in the power of community collaboration and deeply value your contributions.

Whether you're a developer, designer, writer, or user, there are multiple ways where you can make contributions.

## ⭐️ Star

Star and share the project on GitHub:

- [Instill Core](https://github.com/instill-ai/instill-core)

## 👐 Providing Feedback

Head over to our [Community](https://github.com/instill-ai/community) repository, the central hub for discussing our open-source projects, raising issues, and sharing your brilliant ideas.

- 🐞 [File a bug](https://github.com/instill-ai/community/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%F0%9F%90%9B+bug,need-triage&projects=&template=bug_report.yaml&title=%5BBug%5D+%3Ctitle%3E)
- ✨ [Request a feature](https://github.com/instill-ai/community/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%E2%9C%A8+feature%2Cneed-triage&projects=&template=feature_request.yaml&title=%5BFeature%5D+%3Ctitle%3E)
- 🧹 [Suggest an improvement](https://github.com/instill-ai/community/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%F0%9F%A7%B9+improvement%2Cneed-triage&projects=&template=improvement.yaml&title=%5BImprovement%5D+%3Ctitle%3E)
- 📝 [Request a tutorial](https://github.com/instill-ai/community/issues/new?assignees=&labels=tutorial,documentation,need-triage&projects=&template=tutorial_request.yaml&title=%5BTutorial%5D+%3Ctitle%3E)


## 💎 Share Your Work

Sharing how you utilize Instill Core is a form of contribution. It allows others to clone your pipeline and apply it to their own projects.
On Instill Cloud, press **Share** button to share your pipeline (see [here](https://github.com/instill-ai/community/blob/main/hacktoberfest2023/guide-to-sharing-your-pipelines.md#step-5-share-the-pipeline)).

You can choose to **Make the Pipeline Public** or **Share by Link** then press **Copy** Link,
attach a screenshot and share it in the **#show-your-work** channel on [Discord](https://discord.gg/sevxWsqpGh).

## ⚙️ Contributing by Coding

We highly appreciate contributions to our source-available projects in the form of code.
To embark on this collaborative coding journey, please consult the **Contributing Guideline** in each respective codebase repository.

- [Instill Core](https://github.com/instill-ai/instill-core/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- [Instill Console](https://github.com/instill-ai/console/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- [Instill CLI](https://github.com/instill-ai/cli/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- Instill SDKs
  - [Python SDK](https://github.com/instill-ai/python-sdk/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)
  - [TypeScript SDK](https://github.com/instill-ai/typescript-sdk/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)

Additionally, you can learn how to set up your local development environment [here](./contributing/setup-local-development).

## 📖 Contributing to Docs
