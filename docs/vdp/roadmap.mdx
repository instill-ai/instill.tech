VDP is under rapid development. Our team is pushing new features every day to meet the needs of our users. Here is our public high-level roadmap til the end of 2023. If you are interested in what's being worked on right now and what will be explored in the future for the VDP project, please check our [VDP project roadmap](https://github.com/orgs/instill-ai/projects/5/views/4).

## 2023 Q1

- ✅ [api-gateway] Add API Gateway / Reverse Proxy
- ✅ [vdp] Support VDP Kubernetes deployment

## 2023 Q2

- ✅ [Instill Cloud](/docs/instill-cloud/welcome): our hosted service provides you with all the power of VDP, without managing infrastructure
- ✅ [console] No-code, drag-and-drop pipeline builder
- ✅ [pipeline] Flexible and complex pipelines through a DAG design

## 2023 Q3 and Q4

- ✅ [model] Decouple model deployment feature from VDP and introduce a standalone ModelOps platform called **Instill Model**, providing a more stable model inference experience
- 🔮 [connector] Introduce the **AI Connector** to connect AI models from different sources, whether they are third-party model providers or in-house model serving platforms.
  - ✅ Stability AI
  - ✅ OpenAI Text/Chat completion, Whisper, and Embeddings
  - Hugging Face
  - Assembly
- ✅ [connector] Introduce the **Blockchain Connector** to turn digital media files into Web3 assets
- ✅ [vdp] Add a dashboard for out-of-the-box monitoring
- ✅ [vdp] Logging
- 🔌 [connector] Support new data connectors
  - Google Cloud Storage (GCS)
  - AWS S3
  - Snowflake
  - BigQuery
  - VectorDB: Pinecone and Milvus
- 🔌 [operator] free-form operator to manipulate the inputs and outputs of connectors
- 🎯 [pipeline] `PULL` mode for scheduling pipeline
- 🎯 [pipeline] Public pipelines for discovery and sharing
- 🎯 [vdp] Multi-language SDKs
- 🎯 [vdp] Instill CLI

## Contributing to the roadmap

Your feedback is important for us to understand how to build better and more valuable tools for the community.
If you'd like to **send us a feature request**, have a look at the [discussions](https://github.com/instill-ai/vdp/discussions) first.
If your idea has already made it to the planning, give it the thumbs up 👍 to vote for it. If not, you can create a [new discussion](https://github.com/instill-ai/vdp/discussions/new). Besides, you can also join our [community](https://discord.gg/sevxWsqpGh) and request it in the #vdp channel.
